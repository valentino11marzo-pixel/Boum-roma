FAQ

Asked, answered.

Why is the €350 fee non-refundable?
Because the work starts the moment you pay: a senior searcher is assigned, the radar is configured on your criteria, and off-market outreach begins within 24 hours. The fee buys guaranteed work, not a promised outcome — and the search stays active until you sign or tell us to stop.
What if you don't find my apartment?
The search doesn't expire. We keep the radar on and the shortlist flowing until you sign somewhere — with us or without us — or you tell us to stop. You never pay the activation fee twice.
What happens right after payment?
Within 24 hours your radar is live and your searcher has called or messaged to sharpen the brief. The first curated shortlist lands within 72 hours.
Is €350 the only cost?
€350 is the fixed activation fee and it never changes. If you rent a home we source, the standard agency fee applies to that contract — we tell you the exact number before any viewing, never after.
Can I run the whole thing from abroad?
Yes — most PFS ONE clients activate from abroad. Viewings happen live on video, documents are digital, and you can sign the rental contract online before you land in Rome.
Do I have a right of withdrawal?
At checkout you expressly ask us to start the service immediately. Under art. 59 of the Italian Consumer Code, once a service has begun with your express consent, the 14-day withdrawal right no longer applies — which is why we state the non-refundable policy here, in daylight, before you pay.
How is this different from the classic Property Finding page?
Same team, same hunt, same radar. PFS ONE is the productised version: fixed non-refundable fee, instant activation, no expiry — built for people who want to buy a service like they buy anything else online. Prefer the classic flow? It's still at /property-finding.
