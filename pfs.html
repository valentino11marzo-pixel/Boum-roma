Most agencies hide the price behind a phone call. We put the whole policy on the page — including the part other pages bury.

€350 for a studio, €350 for a penthouse. It does not grow with your budget, your zone, or how long the search takes. This number is the entire risk you take on us.

Clausola II — La fee non è rimborsabile

The work starts within 24 hours: a searcher is assigned, the radar is configured, owners get called. By the time a refund could even be discussed, the hours are already spent. We'd rather price that honestly than promise refunds we'd fight. What you buy is guaranteed work — not a promised outcome.

If the right home takes longer, we keep hunting — same searcher, same radar, same fee — until you sign or tell us to stop. You will never be asked to pay the activation twice.

What if you don't find my apartment?+
The search doesn't expire. The radar stays on and the shortlist keeps flowing until you sign — with us or without us — or you tell us to stop. The fee is never charged twice.
Is €350 really the only cost?+
It's the whole fixed fee for the search itself. If you rent a home we source, the standard agency fee applies to that contract — and you hear the exact number before any viewing, never after.

Most clients do. Viewings are live on video, documents are digital, and the rental contract can be signed online before you land in Rome.
