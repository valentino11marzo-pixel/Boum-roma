About Pigneto

Pigneto is Rome's Brooklyn — an authentic, creative neighborhood known for street art, independent cafés, live music, and some of the city's best restaurants. Once a working-class area featured in Pasolini films, Pigneto has evolved into the go-to spot for artists, students, and young professionals who want real Roman life without tourist crowds. Metro access makes it easy to reach the center, but why would you leave when everything you need is here?
