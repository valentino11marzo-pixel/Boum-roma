FAQ

Is the contract check really free?
Yes — the first-pass red-flag review is free and carries no obligation. If you want us to handle the whole deal, that's our Deal Assistance service (€249).
What do you check for?

Usually within 24 hours. If it's urgent, message us on WhatsApp and we'll prioritise it.
