Everything you need to know about renting with BOOM

🔍

What is BOOM?

▼
BOOM is a premium mid-term rental platform (3-24 months) in Rome. We verify every property, handle all paperwork, and provide 24/7 support. Think of us as the Apple of Rome rentals — premium, reliable, no bullshit.

Why should I use BOOM instead of other platforms?

▼
Simple:
• Every apartment is video-verified (no catfishing)
• 24/7 English & Italian support
• Transparent pricing (no hidden fees)
• Professional contracts
• We handle everything from contracts to utilities
• Average placement time: 8 days (vs 45 days market average)

Is BOOM a real estate agency?

▼
Yes. We're Egidi Immobiliare S.r.l., a licensed real estate agency in Rome (P.IVA 17322991005). Fully legal, fully insured, fully compliant with Italian rental law.

What areas of Rome do you cover?

▼
We focus on premium central areas: Trieste, Pigneto, Historic Center, Trastevere, Prati, Monti, San Lorenzo, and surrounding neighborhoods. If you need something in a specific area, use our Property Finding Service.

How do I book an apartment?

▼
1. Browse apartments on our site
2. WhatsApp us to schedule a visit (video tour available)
3. Apply with basic documents
4. Sign contract & pay deposit
5. Move in within 48-72 hours

Total time: 2-5 days average

What documents do I need?

▼
• Valid ID or passport
• Proof of income (last 3 pay slips OR bank statements)
• Reference letter (optional but helpful)
• Codice Fiscale (we help you get one if needed)

That's it. No crazy Italian bureaucracy.

Can I book from abroad?

▼
Yes! We do everything remotely:
• Video tours
• Digital contract signing
• Online payments
• We can even receive your mail/packages before you arrive

What's the Property Finding Service?

▼
For €350 fixed fee, we hunt down your perfect apartment:
• Tell us your requirements
• We search off-market properties
• Present 5 curated options within 24h
• Video tours of all
• Negotiate on your behalf

98% success rate. If we fail, you get €0.

How much do I pay upfront?

▼
Typically:
• First month rent
• 2 months deposit (refundable)
• BOOM commission (1 month rent or 10% annual)

Example: €1,500/month apartment = €1,500 + €3,000 + €1,500 = €6,000 upfront

No hidden fees. Ever.

What payment methods do you accept?

▼
• Bank transfer (preferred)
• Credit/debit card (via Stripe)
• PayPal (on request)

Monthly rent is typically paid by bank transfer.

Are utilities included in the rent?

▼
It depends. Some apartments include utilities (water, gas, electricity), others don't. We always specify this clearly in the listing.

Average utility cost in Rome: €80-150/month depending on usage and season.

When do I get my deposit back?

▼
Your deposit is returned within 30 days after you move out, minus:
• Any damages beyond normal wear & tear
• Unpaid rent or bills
• Professional cleaning (if required)

We do a video inventory at check-in & check-out to avoid disputes.

How does check-in work?

▼
1. We schedule a time (flexible, even weekends/holidays)
2. Meet at the apartment
3. Video inventory walkthrough together
4. Sign inventory report
5. Receive keys & welcome pack
6. We show you how everything works

Takes 30-45 minutes. Airport pickup available for €50.

Can I move in immediately?

▼
If the apartment is listed as "Available Now", yes! Once documents are signed and payment is confirmed, you can move in within 48-72 hours.

What if something breaks?

▼
WhatsApp us. We handle it:
• Normal wear & tear: We fix it, owner pays
• Emergency (leak, no heat, etc): We arrive within 2 hours
• Your fault: You pay for repairs

24/7 support. Always.

Can I extend my lease?

▼
Yes! Just let us know 30 days before your lease ends. If the owner agrees (they usually do), we extend with no extra commission.

Can I leave early?

▼
You need to give 30 days notice. You're responsible for rent until:
• The end of the notice period, OR
• We find a replacement tenant

Whichever comes first. We usually find someone within 7-10 days.

What concierge services do you offer?

▼
• Weekly cleaning: €60/month
• Airport transfer: €50 flat
• Permit help: €150/document
• Mail service: €20/month
• 24/7 emergency: Included

All optional. Pay only for what you use.

How much commission do you charge owners?

▼
1 month rent OR 10% annual (whichever is lower).

In return, you get:
• Professional marketing
• Tenant screening
• Guaranteed rent (we pay even if tenant doesn't)
• 24/7 management
• Legal contracts
• Zero hassle

How fast can you rent my property?

▼
Average: 8 days.

We have a waitlist of verified tenants looking for apartments. Your property won't stay empty.

• Market the property
