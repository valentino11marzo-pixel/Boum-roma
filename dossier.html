SEC. II

The Protocol

Standard operating procedure, four clauses. No exceptions, no small print.

§1Verification on site

Before any home reaches you, we reach it first. An agent inspects the property in person and films the tour — the actual rooms, the actual view, the actual noise at street level. Meanwhile a market radar sweeps new listings every fifteen minutes and AI matching scores each home against what you asked for, so nothing worth seeing arrives late.

Filed Video tour, on-site notes, radar timestamp. Sweep interval: 15 min.

§2Application, two minutes

One short form, in English, from your phone. No folder of notarised photocopies, no queue at an office that closes at noon. You state who you are and when you are arriving; the file does the rest of the walking.

Noted Median completion: under 2 minutes. Documents parsed automatically.

§3The contract, signed legally from your phone

A real Italian lease, registered and enforceable — and explained to you in plain English, clause by clause, before you sign. Magic Sign puts the signature on your phone screen; the law puts it on the record. You know exactly what you agreed to, which in this market qualifies as a plot twist.

Instrument Magic Sign — phone-signed, legally binding, bilingual walkthrough included.

§4Keys in forty-eight hours

From accepted application to keys in hand: forty-eight hours. Your lease and documents live in a realtime tenant portal; a pass in your Apple Wallet gets you oriented. The case closes; the apartment opens.

Deadline 48h, tracked. Tenant portal + Apple Wallet pass issued at handover.
