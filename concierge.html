Why BOOM exists
Why BOOM exists

Rome was never meant to be rented through a spreadsheet.

For decades, finding a home in Rome as an outsider has meant the same friction: cash deposits in unfamiliar currencies, contracts in a language you don't yet speak, landlords who won't sign without an Italian guarantor you don't have.

BOOM was built for the people the system forgot — students, researchers, expats, and professionals who arrive with intent and deserve a clean way in.

We are a concierge, not a portal. We screen properties, draft compliant Allegato B contracts, register them with the Agenzia delle Entrate, and stand behind every tenant when they need it.

We say no when we should. A 3-week stay belongs on Airbnb. A €700 budget in central Rome is a misalignment we'd rather flag than dress up.

Egidi Immobiliare S.r.l. — Rome, since 2024.
