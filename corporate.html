FAQ

Good to know.

How much does corporate relocation to Rome cost?
Founding rates while we build our track record — a fraction of the €1,500–€5,000 agencies charge. Soft Landing from €990 (pay on move-in); Full Relocation from €1,980; Managed Corporate Housing custom. Volume discounts from 5 relocations a year, plus a money-back promise if we don’t find a home. Prices exclude VAT and the rent/deposit paid to the landlord.
Do you invoice companies with VAT?
Yes. BOOM is operated by Egidi Immobiliare S.r.l. (P.IVA 17322991005) and issues proper VAT invoices — one consolidated invoice across multiple relocations if you prefer.
Can you relocate an employee before they arrive in Italy?
Yes. Remote live video viewings plus a refundable hold let an employee secure a verified apartment from their home country, so keys are ready on day one.
Do you handle Italian bureaucracy?
Yes — the Full Relocation package covers codice fiscale, residence/permesso guidance, utilities and a bank account introduction, all in English.
We relocate people regularly — is there a volume option?
From 5 relocations a year you get tiered discounts and a dedicated account manager; an annual "always-on" retainer unlocks member pricing, priority placement and an SLA. No onboarding cost, no exclusivity.
How fast can someone move in?
As little as 48 hours for a verified apartment, depending on the employee's requirements and timeline.
