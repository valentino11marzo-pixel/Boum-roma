“One BOOM.” The single source of truth. Every page imports /css/boom-system.css; nothing else defines these tokens. Primitives are namespaced .bx-* — a page opts in by using a class, so importing this changes nothing until we choose to. This page is the contract: what you see here is law across home, list and detail.
