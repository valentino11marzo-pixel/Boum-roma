About Trieste / Coppedè

The Trieste and Coppedè neighborhoods offer a unique blend of residential tranquility and architectural wonder. Famous for the fairy-tale Quartiere Coppedè with its Art Nouveau and Liberty-style buildings, this area is well-connected by Metro B and perfect for students and professionals. You'll find universities nearby, excellent restaurants, and all daily services within walking distance — all while enjoying one of Rome's most photogenic districts.
