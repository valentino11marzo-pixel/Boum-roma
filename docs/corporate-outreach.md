## Rome is a corporate-relocation goldmine (target top to bottom)

**1. UN & international organisations (HQ'd in Rome — constant inbound staff):**
- **FAO** (Food & Agriculture Organization), **WFP** (World Food Programme),
  **IFAD** — thousands of international staff, consultants and interns rotating
  through Rome. Target: HR / Staff Welfare / Onboarding / Duty-of-care teams.
- **Embassies & cultural institutes** (US, UK, German, French, Australian…),
  **ICCROM**, **Unidroit**, NATO Defense College. Incoming diplomatic & admin
  staff need trusted, English-first housing.

**2. Film, TV & production (international crews, weeks–months):**
- **Cinecittà Studios** + the production companies shooting in Rome (Netflix,
  HBO, Apple TV+ shoots). Crews and cast need furnished mid-term housing *fast*.
  Target: line producers, production coordinators, location managers.

**3. Multinationals & their Italy HR / global-mobility teams:**
- Rome-based: **Eni, Leonardo, TIM, Poste Italiane, Cisco Italy, IBM, Accenture,
  Deloitte/EY/KPMG/PwC** (Rome offices), pharma, aerospace.
- Any company opening a Rome office or sending a team — watch LinkedIn for
  “expanding to Rome / hiring in Rome”.

**4. Relocation / DSP agencies (channel partners — white-label your service):**
- Destination-service providers and global RMCs that get Rome assignments but
  have no strong local Rome partner. Offer them BOOM as their on-the-ground
  Rome arm. One agency = many relocations.

**5. Scale-ups & tech hiring internationally**, **sports clubs** (foreign
players/staff), **fashion houses**, **gaming studios**.

## The pitch (problem → BOOM → proof → ask)

1. **Problem they feel:** a relocating employee lost in Rome's rental market =
   delayed start, scam risk, your duty-of-care exposed, HR time drained.
2. **BOOM:** one partner for the whole move — verified home, lease, deposit,
   utilities, codice fiscale, residence — English-first, one VAT invoice.
3. **Proof / trust (you're new, so earn it):** registered Italian agency (Egidi
   Immobiliare S.r.l., P.IVA 17322991005), legal contracts, 4.9★, **pay-on-move-in
   + money-back guarantee** so they risk nothing trying you once.
4. **Ask:** “Could we handle your next Rome relocation as a no-risk pilot?”
