# BOOM · Owner / landlord acquisition (the supply side)

**Why this is half the business:** you can win all the tenant demand in Rome,
but without **verified apartments to offer**, you can't convert it. Every new
quality owner = inventory that fuels every other channel. Owners are also a
*recurring* relationship: one landlord with 3 flats, re-let every year, is an
annuity. **Treat supply acquisition as seriously as demand.**

**The page to send:** **https://www.boomrome.com/owners**
**Core promise to owners:** *international, verified tenants · zero hassle ·
legal & compliant · reliable income — we manage everything.*

---

## Who to target (Rome, top to bottom)

1. **Private landlords already self-listing** on Immobiliare.it, Idealista,
   Subito, Facebook Marketplace/groups — they're clearly motivated but doing it
   the hard way (vetting, no-shows, language, paperwork). Easiest "yes".
2. **Owners of empty / underused flats** — inherited apartments, second homes,
   flats stuck on long-let. Pitch mid-term: higher yield than 12-month, less
   churn/risk than short-let (and no CIN/short-let crackdown headaches).
3. **Ex-Airbnb / short-let hosts** squeezed by Rome's short-let rules — offer
   mid-term as the compliant, lower-effort alternative.
4. **Amministratori di condominio & small property managers** — one contact =
   many units. High leverage.
5. **Expat / overseas owners** who own a Rome flat but live abroad — they
   desperately want a trustworthy local manager who operates in English.

---

## The pitch (owner version)

1. **Their pain:** vetting strangers, no-shows, unpaid rent, damage, language
   barriers, bureaucracy, void months.
2. **BOOM:** we bring **pre-verified international tenants** (students,
   professionals, researchers, sent by universities & companies), handle the
   **legal contract, deposit, payments and management**, and keep the flat
   occupied with good people. You get reliable income, zero hassle.
3. **Trust (you're new):** registered agency (Egidi Immobiliare S.r.l., P.IVA
   17322991005), legal contracts, transparent terms. Start with **one flat** as
   a no-pressure trial.
4. **Ask:** "Can we place a verified tenant in your apartment this season?"

## Cadence & mechanics

- **Daily habit:** 10 private listings/day on Immobiliare + Idealista → short,
  respectful message. Volume + speed of reply is the edge.
- **Lead with one flat, no exclusivity.** A new agency earns trust by delivering
  one great placement, not by asking for a portfolio.
- **Seasonality:** push **June–September** (student/academic + corporate intake)
  and **December–January** (spring arrivals) — that's when you have the tenant
  demand to fill their flat fast.
- **Track** with the UTM link; owner leads land via the `owners.html` form.
