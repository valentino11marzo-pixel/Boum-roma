# BOOM · University & study-abroad outreach (the #1 free channel)

**Why:** every semester, hundreds of international students arrive in Rome and
*must* find housing fast — it's the #1 complaint their housing/international
offices deal with, and students get scammed constantly. BOOM is exactly the
trusted, English-first, anti-scam solution those offices want to recommend.
Win **5–6 offices**, not 5,000 students. Pre-qualified, seasonal, high-intent.

**Timing:** front-load NOW. Fall intake decisions happen **May–Aug**; spring
intake **Nov–Dec**. Be on their "recommended housing" list before the wave.

---

"Student Services – Rome" / on-site coordinator. For ESN: their IG/FB DM or
board email.

## The partner offer (no cost to them — that's the point)
- **Free to recommend.** You're a vetted, licensed Italian agency
  (*Egidi Immobiliare S.r.l.*) with English contracts and 48h move-in.
- **Student-friendly:** priority response, video viewings from abroad,
  transparent all-in pricing, **a student discount or a campaign code** per partner.
- **Anti-scam = their pain solved:** every listing video-verified; you protect
  students from the fake-listing/deposit scams that plague them.
- **Trackable:** each office gets its own UTM link/code so they (and you) see the
  value — and you can offer a **referral fee or scholarship contribution** for
  placements if useful.
- **Make it easy:** offer a one-page PDF flyer + a link they can paste on their
  housing page / orientation email / welcome pack.
