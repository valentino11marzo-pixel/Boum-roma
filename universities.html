FAQ

Good to know.

Does partnering cost the university or our students anything?
Becoming a partner is free, with no exclusivity. Browsing apartments and applying is free for students. Students only pay if they choose an optional paid service — for example a €89 remote video viewing — and even that is entirely their choice.
Is BOOM a legitimate, registered company?
Yes. BOOM is operated by Egidi Immobiliare S.r.l. (P.IVA 17322991005), based in Rome. Every rental uses a legal contract with a properly handled, protected deposit.
Can students secure housing before they arrive in Italy?
Yes. Remote live video viewings plus a refundable hold let a student lock a verified apartment from their home country, before flying to Rome.
What neighbourhoods and budgets do you cover?
All central and student-friendly Rome neighbourhoods — from San Lorenzo and Trastevere to Prati and Monti — across a range of budgets. We match each student to what fits their program location and budget.
Do you help students with Italian bureaucracy?
Yes. Our concierge guides students through the essentials such as codice fiscale, utilities and settling in — all in English.
How do we get started?
Send the form above or email us. We set up your office in about 15 minutes with a referral link, ready-to-share materials and a named contact.
