FAQ

Good to know.

Is this for apartments listed on BOOM?
No — live video tours of BOOM's own homes are always free, just ask from any listing page. This service is for apartments you found anywhere else: Immobiliare, Idealista, Facebook, any agency.
How fast can you do a viewing?
Usually within 24–48 hours of payment, scheduled directly with the landlord or agency.
What exactly do I receive?
A live video tour you direct, 24+ HD photos, a condition report, a neighbourhood check and our honest recommendation — including what we didn't like.
Does it work for apartments not listed on BOOM?
Yes — any apartment in Rome, from any portal or agency. Send us the link and we take it from there.
Which platforms do you use?
Zoom, WhatsApp or FaceTime — whatever's easiest for you, from anywhere in the world. The call is recorded and it's yours.
What if the apartment isn't good?
We tell you honestly, with reasons. Far better €89 spent than a bad deposit committed.
What if you can't reach the property?
Full refund, no questions. And if you later rent the home through BOOM, the €89 is credited to your agency fee.
