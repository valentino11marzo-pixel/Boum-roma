FAQ

Good to know.

Are your fees grant-eligible and invoiceable?
Yes. BOOM (Egidi Immobiliare S.r.l., P.IVA 17322991005) issues proper VAT invoices that fit relocation/mobility allowance and institutional relocation budgets under ERC, Marie Skłodowska-Curie (MSCA), Horizon Europe and PNRR. We can invoice the researcher or the institution.
Our researchers are on fixed-term contracts — is that a problem for landlords?
No. We work with landlords used to mid-term, fixed-period stays and structure the lease accordingly, so a 2–4 year grant is not an obstacle.
Can you help a researcher relocating with family?
Yes. Our Family Landing package adds a school shortlist, family settling-in and partner support, so the whole household lands smoothly.
Can a fellow secure housing before arriving in Italy?
Yes. Remote video viewings and a refundable hold let a researcher lock a verified apartment from abroad, with keys ready on arrival.
Is the institutional partnership really free?
Yes. Partnering is free with no exclusivity. Researchers (or the institution) only pay for the relocation package they choose.
How do we get started?
Send the form above. Whether you're a researcher, an HR/research office or a grant manager, we'll reply within 24 hours with next steps and grant-ready invoicing details.
